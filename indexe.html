Introduction

34 is a rice noodle brand under PHUONG HONG DIEU COMPANY LIMITED.

Headquartered in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, we specialize in the production and export of traditional Vietnamese foods such as rice vermicelli, pho noodles, hu tieu, and many other products.

With a strong commitment to quality, food safety, and authentic Vietnamese taste, we offer both fresh and dried products, made from carefully selected ingredients and processed with modern hygiene standards.

At 34, we aim to bring the original flavor of Vietnam to customers all around the world.
